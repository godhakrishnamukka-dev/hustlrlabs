05 — FAQ
You actually build apps? +

Yes — it's our main thing. Ordering apps, booking apps, loyalty apps, internal dashboards — if your business needs it, we build it, ship it, and maintain it. Tell us the idea and we'll tell you exactly what it takes.

How much does it cost? +

It depends on what you need — a landing page is a very different job from a full brand + automation setup. Message us what you're after and you'll get a fixed quote within 24 hours. Student-run means our rates are genuinely small-business friendly.

Do I need to know anything about tech? +

Nope. You tell us about your business in plain words, we handle everything technical. We'll explain what we're doing without the jargon, and you approve things on WhatsApp as we go.

How long does a project take? +

Small jobs like a Google Business setup or a landing page can be done within days. Full websites and brand identities usually take 1–3 weeks. You get a timeline upfront with your quote, and updates while we work.

Who owns the work when it's done? +

You do. Your accounts, your website, your logo files — everything is handed over in your name with full access. We're happy to keep maintaining it for you, but you're never locked in.

What happens after launch? +

Your choice: take the keys and run it yourself, or have us stay on for content, maintenance and management month to month. No long contracts either way.

You're students — can I trust you with my business? +

Fair question. Being students is exactly why we're good at this: we grew up on these platforms and we're building our reputation, which means we can't afford to do sloppy work. Fixed scope, written plans, and you own everything — so the risk stays low and the work stays sharp.
